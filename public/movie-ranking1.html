Best Movies according to me

My Top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

The film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for mankind.

Zindagi Na Millegi Dobara

The movie explores themes of friendship, love, marriage, and work. It also teaches the lesson that life is short and should be lived to the fullest.

Kehvatlal Parivar

Kehvatlal Parivar is a story of an extraordinarily crazy family who despite the difficulties and differences they always stick together.
